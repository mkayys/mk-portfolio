About Me

Hello, my name is Michelle and although I am a software developer now, I started with a background in Psychology. Why? Because I loved learning how people think. We are all unique in our way of solving problems and how we process the world around us. I've always been curious and enjoyed collaborating with others because I truly believe more is achieved with many minds coming together.

With that said, while working at a medical device company, I came to realize there were qualities that I wanted in a job that weren't happening for me. I wanted an opportunity to learn and grow. I also wanted to have a chance to be creative and have input on what the company was doing.

This led me to coding. I feel there is a misconception about coding. Although the ideas that computer science is technical, analytical, and logical is not incorrect, software development requires a lot of creativity and begins with questions. It was a perfect fit for what I was looking for. I wanted to be challenged but also have the opportunity to be inquisitive, resulting in growth.
